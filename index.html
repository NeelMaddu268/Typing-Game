Typing Test

This is a typing test. You will be given a word. You will have to type it out as fast as you can.

After typing the word, you will get another one and a point will be added to your score.

Selecting the Easy difficulty will not add any challenges to the game.
Selecting the Hard difficulty will reduce the time by 5 seconds for every wrong letter typed.
